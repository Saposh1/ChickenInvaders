WELCOME TO CHICKEN INVADERS!

Your Goal: Killing all the chickens on the screen. A chicken from the first row worth 5, second row 10, etc.

Be carefull of the fried eggs!! You have 3 lives so don't waste them!

After logging, in the setting page, you are able to choose a key for shooting and desired gaming time.

Be aware that diagonal movement is available by pressing both key in the desired direction.

More over, Chickens and fried eggs moving diagonally, as well as your shooting bullets! Cool!

In the top of the game screen you can find countdown timer, scores counter and remaining lives counter.
